Control Theory & Physics

The AI Controller solves the Bellman Equation to find the optimal policy $\pi^*(x)$ that minimizes cost over the horizon:

$$ J(x_t) = \min_{u_t} \left\{ C(x_t, u_t) + J(x_{t+1}) \right\} $$

Subject to the Power Balance Equation:

$$ P_{grid} + P_{renewable} + P_{battery} = P_{load} $$

Where $u_t$ is the battery action and $x_t$ is the State of Charge (SoC).
